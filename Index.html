A Singer's Dream

We all start somewhere they say.
why not with dreams and aspirations im sure someday,
a face will be put to your voice on display.
But hard work and determination is the price to pay.

'Can I?'
she conveys.
A singer's dream painted gray.
we all start somewhere they say.

Praises to her ambitions and courage,
a face put to a voice on stage.
as the adage,
"we all start somewhere they say"

Echoes of Time

A ticking clock, a fading sound,
Echoes lost, yet never found.
Moments drifting, like dust in air,
Slipping through hands, unaware.
